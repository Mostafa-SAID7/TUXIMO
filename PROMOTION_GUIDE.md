## ⚠️ What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't buy fake stars** - Against GitHub ToS, will get banned
❌ **Don't spam** - Hurts reputation
❌ **Don't beg for stars** - Looks desperate
❌ **Don't use bots** - Will be detected and removed

## ✅ Legitimate Growth Only

Focus on:
- ✅ Quality code
- ✅ Great documentation
- ✅ Solving real problems
- ✅ Community engagement
- ✅ Consistent updates
- ✅ Helpful responses
